Boss Fight
January 2024 - Present
10-20 hours
900+ lines
My most recent game, but very much a work in progress so far. I used my boid algorithm for boss movement around walls. I also created an ellipse collision function for a “Casket Toss” attack by the ranged boss. Finally, I used a Calculus BC principle called a "Geometric Series" to calculate the initial speed the casket should be thrown at and how quickly this speed should decay. This ensures the casket lands at the player's position every time while following a believable, decaying parabolic path to get there. Melee boss and better graphics coming soon!

Tic Tac Toe
June 2023
20+ hours
900+ lines
Using a very buggy hand detection model on OpenCV to play TicTacToe. Originally this was a project to build a BFS/DFS based Tic-Tac-Toe bot for the 8-week AI course I was attending at UTD. After quickly finishing that and having copious amounts of time left, I thought why not make the controller cooler. A few hours of debugging later I was left with an OpenCV model that could detect my hand using my laptop's camera to let me play TicTacToe against a custom BFS bot! Using OpenCV's hand detection model, I created a mouse pointer that is located between the user's index finger and thumb. To click, the user has to pinch their fingers in a similar way to Apple's recent "Apple Vision Pro" product.
