Triangle Background
July 2023
20+ hours
371 lines
On Sebastian Lague's youtube channel I found a really cool looking triangle tesselation background and wanted to see if I could code it myself. After spending an hour or so researching Delaunay Triangulation I coded my own implementation of it and began experimenting with color combinations and other ways to make it look even more interesting. Another super cool fact I found about Delaunay Triangulation is that it can be used to generate Voronoi Diagrams by connecting the centers of the circumcircles of each triangle.

Battle Grounds
December 2022 - April 2023
5-10 hours
377 lines
Using new particle classes I wrote for my CSP Project (look in the games section), I decided to make an explosion graphic. The color palette and general design of the explosion was based on DaFluffyPotato's game “Explon't.” Watch his video here: Youtube Link. One thing I've noticed is that the particles clump up at tile boundaries; this is why the smoke appears to be in vertical lines rather than uniformly spread out. At the time I didn't know how to solve this, but I've fixed it in recent particle class iterations by splitting the vertical and horizontal collision checks. I left the older particle class version in this video demo to show how I've improved!
